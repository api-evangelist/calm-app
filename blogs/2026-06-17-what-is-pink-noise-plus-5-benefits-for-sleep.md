Describes pink noise as reducing higher frequencies and emphasizing lower ones for a soothing rainfall-like effect, and lists five sleep benefits including masking sound, supporting deeper sleep, and reducing stress. Notes one study suggested it may negatively impact insight and creativity, and that it is not a cure for disorders like sleep apnea.
