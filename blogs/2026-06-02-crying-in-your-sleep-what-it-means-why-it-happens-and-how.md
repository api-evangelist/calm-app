Experiencing tears during sleep is a common phenomenon affecting many adults, particularly during emotionally challenging periods. Crying in your sleep often mirrors the intricate mesh of your emotional and mental state, reflecting the brain's efforts to process unresolved feelings. The article identifies various triggers including stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, and vivid nightmares, while offering practical strategies like relaxation techniques, mindfulness practices, and professional support.
